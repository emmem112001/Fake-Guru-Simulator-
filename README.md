# Fake-Guru-Simulator
🎮 Fake Guru Simulator — Game Description
Fake Guru Simulator is a satirical simulation game where you build an online persona and try to grow influence, wealth, and attention. You start as an unknown creator and make strategic decisions to grow your followers, earn money, and maintain credibility.
Every action has consequences. Posting hype content may boost your popularity quickly, but it reduces trust. Selling courses increases income but damages credibility. Building genuine value improves trust but grows more slowly.
The challenge is to balance short-term success with long-term survival. If your credibility drops too low, you get exposed and the game ends. If you run out of money or lose all followers, your journey collapses.
There are no fixed paths—only trade-offs. Your choices determine whether you become a respected creator or a failed internet persona.
